## Risk of Rain 2
![Risk of Rain 2](https://cdn.cloudflare.steamstatic.com/steam/apps/632360/header.jpg)

**Risk of Rain 2** is one of my favorite games. It’s fast-paced, easy to play and scales in difficulty the longer you play.

### Why I Like It
- The gameplay becomes *insanely fast* as you stack items and the enemies get stronger and ramp up
- Every run feels different due to randomization
- Has one of my **favorite** game soundtracks

> "The longer you stay, the harder it gets."
